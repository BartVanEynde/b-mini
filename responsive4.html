Resize window to collapse menu

It’s quite common to have burger menus to replace standard horizontal menus on small viewports. This way, the menu becomes completely responsive and provides the best experience depending on the viewport size. If that’s what you are looking for, this example will do exactly that, and with only CSS. To test it out, open the codepen in a new window and resize the result panel. In responsive mode, the hamburger menu will open the list of items one after the other in a vertical column coming from the top. Quite a standard behavior for mobile devices.
